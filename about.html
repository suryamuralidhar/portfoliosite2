About Me

Architectural graduate specializing in Interior Design and Architectural Visualization. I enjoy translating architectural concepts into calm, balanced spatial experiences.

My work focuses on neutral palettes, natural materials, soft lighting and thoughtful proportions to create spaces that feel peaceful and timeless.

I believe interior design should bring clarity, comfort and emotional calm to everyday environments.

Design Philosophy

Clarity

Balanced proportions and simple spatial composition create calm environments that feel effortless.

Materiality

Natural materials and subtle textures bring warmth, depth and authenticity to interior spaces.

Function

Good design should quietly support daily life while maintaining elegance and simplicity.

Tools I Work With

AutoCAD Revit 3ds Max Corona Renderer SketchUp Lumion D5 Render Photoshop

Beyond Design

Outside architecture I enjoy exploring electronics, new technologies and experimental DIY projects.

I am also interested in Amateur Radio (Ham Radio), a technical hobby that explores wireless communication systems and signal experimentation.

Amateur Radio

Amateur Radio is a global technical hobby where operators communicate through radio frequencies without relying on internet networks.

It combines electronics, signal propagation and communication technology.

My interest in Ham Radio reflects curiosity toward wireless communication and engineering.
